Campus Wellness

Campus Wellness provides medical care and mental health services to Waterloo students.

Professionals like physicians and nurses can give students health care and help them work through barriers causing distress using an "inclusive, connected, and collaborative approach."

The Health Services building is across from the Student Life Centre (SLC). The Counselling Services are at Needles Hall North, 2nd floor.

Please visit here.

*Note that counselling services individual appointments are available at NO CHARGE.

Services Offered

Health Services:

  • Urgent Care
  • Walk-In Visits
  • Nutrition Services
  • Laboratory Services
  • Birth Control
  • Emergency Contraception
  • STI Testing
  • Pregnancy Tests
  • Immunizations

Counselling Services:

  • Individual Counselling
  • Crisis Intervention
  • Therapy Groups
  • Coping Skills Seminars
  • Workshops
