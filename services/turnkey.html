Turnkey Desks

The Turnkey Desks will answer any and all questions you have about whats going on around campus or the local community. In addition to the services detialed below, if you have any general inquries about UW the Turnkey Desks are here to help.

There is one located in the Student Life Centre Great Hall, and another one located in Davis Centre.

The Turnkey Desk is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year and has operated almost continuously since 1968.
