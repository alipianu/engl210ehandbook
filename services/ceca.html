Centre for Career Action

The Centre for Career Action is here to help students with all their career development and job search needs.

They offer one-on-one appointments with a career advisor who will help students with their career plan, job search strategies, and post-grad planning.

Services Offered

Career Hub

A website that helps students write their résumé

Appointments and workshops

Students can book an appointment with a career advisor or participate in workshops.
Please visit here for a calendar of all events.

Career Action Library

Contains print, video, and computer-based resources for career planning.

Job listings

Offers job opportunities, including full-time, co-op and part-time jobs. They also offer volunteering opportunities.

Employer Information Sessions

Organized by Hire Waterloo, students can attend all sorts of Employer Information Sessions (EIS) in order to find job opportunities and to get in touch of big compagnies.

For more information, please visit here.
