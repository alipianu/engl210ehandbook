Student Government

Feds

Every University of Waterloo undergraduate student is registered as a member of Feds, or the Federation of Students. Feds is the student union at the University of Waterloo whose goal is to represent and advocate for the needs of undergraduate students at the university. Would you like to get involved and become a representative for your fellow students? Find out how to run for one of the Feds' elected positions here.
